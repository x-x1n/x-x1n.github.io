Communication and Technology Project

This is the group project of the course Communication and Technology.
It demonstrates various thoughts of the design of a video game with the theme of ‘technology and progress, convenience, bodily needs and communication’, through the form of a brochure.
Was commented as: ‘This project on “Technology within a Social Context” stood out for its extremely impressive output, exploring the chosen concept with depth and clarity. ’ by the teacher

Due to limited time, we were unable to make it out as a real game.
However, we did build up the imagined scenes with the help of digital tools, and we also completed the writing of overall plan.
The game requires player to decipher a fictive language to communicate with local habitants and find a way to leave the island. With the progression of the game, different technological tools will be unlocked to support players’ deciphering. Through the adopting of different technology, the game critically asks the players to examine the relationship between technology and communication.
The idea was proposed by me. During the making of the project, I was responsible for the majority part of mechanism design, text writing and group arrangements. The layout (including the design of icons and brochure logo) was done all by myself, and I also participated in the building of scenes in Blender.

Screen and Popular Culture Project

This is the work of Screen and Popular Culture in UNNC. The course requires students to make a creative project in any form that is related to given topics with an exegesis.
The project focuses on sociological concepts of Self, Persona and Commodity, and further explores the status quo of individuals in the contemporary networked societal environment.
